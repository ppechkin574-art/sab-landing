What we do

Two directions, one company

We operate at the intersection of technology and trade, leveraging each to strengthen the other.

Mobile Application Development

We design and build native mobile applications for iOS and Android, from concept and UX to App Store and Google Play publication. Our focus is on business tools, commerce platforms, and consumer products tailored for the Central Asian market.

  • iOS applications (Swift, SwiftUI)
  • Android applications (Kotlin, Jetpack Compose)
  • Cross-platform solutions (React Native, Flutter)
  • App Store & Google Play publication
  • Product design and technical support

Wholesale Trade

Wholesale trade across a broad product mix (OKED 46909) throughout the Republic of Kazakhstan. We build long-term partnerships with suppliers and clients through transparent terms and reliable delivery.

  • Diversified product portfolio
  • B2B partnerships and supply contracts
  • Logistics within Kazakhstan
  • Transparent pricing and documentation
  • Full compliance with KZ regulations
About

A new-generation company from Kazakhstan

SARY ARQA BELT, TOO was incorporated on 5 January 2023 in the Republic of Kazakhstan. Our registered office is located in the Pavlodar Region, and our operations span the entire country.

We combine two complementary directions: software engineering — in particular mobile application development — and wholesale trade. This duality allows us to both build digital tools and put them to use inside real operating businesses.

Our approach is built on reliability, transparent terms of cooperation, and strict adherence to the laws of the Republic of Kazakhstan. We are open to new partnerships, development projects, and supplier relationships.

Our mission
To bring modern technology into everyday business across Central Asia — through software and trade alike.
Our values
Integrity, long-term thinking, engineering quality, and respect for legal and cultural context.
